Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

AttendCount ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the AttendCount Progressive Web App.

1. Information We Collect

Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile photo. We use your email address solely to identify your account and scope your attendance data. We do not sell, share, or use your email for marketing.

Attendance Data: We store the attendance records you create within the app (subjects, daily logs, and baselines) in a secure Supabase database. This data is associated with your Google email and is only accessible to you.

Error Logs: If the app encounters an error, we may log a minimal error message and device information (browser user-agent) to help us diagnose issues. These logs do not contain personal data beyond your email.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the attendance tracking service.
  • To identify your account and keep your data isolated from other users.
  • To send class reminder notifications (with your explicit permission).
  • To diagnose technical errors and improve app stability.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II certified cloud platform, using PostgreSQL with Row-Level Security enabled. This means your data is cryptographically isolated — no query can ever return another user's data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with any third party except as required by law.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will permanently delete all your attendance records, subjects, and baselines within 30 days of your request.

6. Children's Privacy

AttendCount is designed for university students (typically aged 18+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to delete your account, please contact us through the app or at our support email.
